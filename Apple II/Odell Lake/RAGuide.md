This guide will contain all the possible valid interaction in the game. If there is more than one letter it means that both are valid options.

Any additional info or correction can be sent to me in a DM to my [RA account](https://retroachievements.org/user/Maximdraco).

***
# Actions Guide
|Action|Key|Description|
|:--|:--|:--|
|**EAT**|E|Your fish attempts to eat the oncoming creature.
|**CHASE AWAY**|C|Your fish attempts to chase the oncoming creature.
|**IGNORE**|I|Your fish completely ignores the oncoming creature.
|**SHALLOW ESCAPE**|S|Your fish flees to shallow water.
|**DEEP ESCAPE**|D|Your fish flees to deeper water.
